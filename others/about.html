Our Mission

At Six Way Studio, we noticed that simple tasks online often require complex solutions. Want to convert a file? You are bombarded with ads. Need to compress an image? You have to upload it to a random server.

We created Mokhles to change that. We are building a suite of everyday tools that respect your time and your privacy.

No paywalls. No hidden data collection. Just clean, functional code.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mokhles really free?

Yes, completely. Mokhles is an open-source project and 100% free to use. To keep our servers running and support ongoing development, we generate revenue through unobtrusive Google Ads.

Do you store my uploaded files?

No. For tools like image compressors or converters, the processing happens inside your browser. Your files never leave your device.
